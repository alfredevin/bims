About Us

Empowering Barangay Sumangga through Digital Innovation

The Barangay Information Management System (BIMS) is designed to streamline barangay operations and improve service delivery to the residents of Sumangga, Mogpog, Marinduque. With this system, we aim to enhance transparency, efficiency, and accessibility of local government services.

Mission

To provide efficient, accessible, and transparent services through modern technology, ensuring the welfare and development of every resident of Barangay Sumangga.

Vision

A digitally empowered barangay that upholds good governance, fosters community development, and delivers quality public service to all constituents.
